About Web Dev Reds

The Web Development Club at Illinois State University is a student-led organization made up of passionate members from the IT department. We meet weekly to expand our knowledge, sharpen our skills, and explore the latest in web development technologies. Whether you're just starting out or looking to level up your expertise, our club offers a collaborative space to learn, build, and grow together in the world of web development.

Why join?

Do you have an interest in web development or want to learn more about coding and building websites? Well, then this is the club for you! At the Web Development Club, we will guide you through the process of building websites no matter what experience level you are at. Here you can learn to build websites, talk to other web devs, or just chill. Join the Web Development Club!
